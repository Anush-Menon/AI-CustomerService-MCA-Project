## Project Overview
This research project evaluates the extent to which Artificial Intelligence 
improves or diminishes customer service quality, satisfaction, and response 
efficiency in technology companies.

**Research Type:** Mixed-Methods (Quantitative + Qualitative)  
**Sample Size:** 120 respondents (60 CS Professionals + 60 End Customers)  
**Key Metrics:** CSAT, FCR Rate, AHT, Net Promoter Score (NPS)

---

## Key Findings
- Strong positive correlation between AI adoption and all 4 outcome metrics (r > 0.71, p < 0.001)
- CSAT improved from **2.81 (No AI)** to **4.31 (High AI adoption)** — a 53% increase
- **67.5%** cited contextual understanding failure as AI's biggest limitation
- **51.7%** prefer a hybrid AI + human escalation model
